Birthday Cake
Ingredients:
  • 2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ cups granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 ¼ cups milk
  • 2 ½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
Directions:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 °F. Grease and flour two 8″ cake pans.
  2. Whisk flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Mix sugar & eggs until fluffy; add vanilla.
  4. Alternate adding dry mix and milk, beginning and ending with dry.
  5. Divide batter and bake 25–30 min, until toothpick is clean.
  6. Cool 10 min in pans, then transfer to wire racks.
